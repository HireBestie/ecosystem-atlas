Build with Claude — Evaluation Kit

A complete, reusable pack for a six-week portfolio or accelerator cohort. Purpose: move teams from curiosity to an evidence-backed go/no-go on Claude for a real workflow — then measure activation.

This is a programme prototype, not evidence of a completed Motier or Station F engagement.

1. Cohort design 2. Qualification 3. Briefing 4. Eval template 5. Rubric 6. Cost worksheet 7. Office hours 8. Activation 9. Follow-up 10. Feedback & ROI

What this kit proves

  • Partner selection → technical enablement → activation → product feedback → ROI can be operated as one motion.
  • Enablement is inspectable (templates, rubric, worksheet), not a slide claim.
  • Honest no-fit outcomes are first-class — technical trust before expansion.

Suggested six-week arc

  • Week 0–1: Partner owner + qualification + intake
  • Week 2: Founder briefing + case drafting
  • Week 3–4: Baseline runs + rubric scoring + office hours
  • Week 5: Go / no-go decisions documented
  • Week 6 + 30 days: Activation threshold + follow-up + feedback notes
